About Me

I was born in Kyoto, Japan in 1993 and moved to the United States when I was just a year old. Growing up in Florida, I was raised by my Colombian relatives, which allowed me to pick up some Spanish along the way. My life has always been fueled by a deep fascination with technology. I've not only built my own computer but also enjoyed working on my own car and developing various coding projects. Currently, I hold the position of Vice President at UCF's Design and Code Club. In this role, I assist the President as needed, instruct workshop tutorials, search for event speakers, and brainstorm innovative ideas to help foster and expand our community.
